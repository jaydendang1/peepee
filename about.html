Who we are

Welcome to HeavenlySnaccs. A place where we can explore different snacks from South East Asia (Japan, Vietnam and China). We are an online business that sells international snacks to Australian consumers. Providing Australians a unique opportunity to explore the world through the taste of their tongues.

We understand that a lot of us are unable to travel to other places hence why our services offer the best customer experience, bringing a whole countries flavour to our fellow Australians right at their doorstep. What makes us stand out from other online businesses is that we give away these samples for free, and all you have to cover for is shipping!

Our team is very welcoming and works very hard to provide the best customer service, as our employees are always ready and available to help you.
